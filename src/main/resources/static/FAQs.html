GetLoudTix

FAQs

1. What is GetLoudTix?
GetLoudTix is an online ticket reservation platform for free concerts, allowing users to reserve tickets easily and securely.

2. Do I need an account to make a reservation?
No, but you need to sign in and provide information such as name and email for your identity.

3. Is there a fee to reserve tickets?
No, all reservations through GetLoudTix are free.

4. How do I make a reservation?
Click “Start Reservation” on the homepage.
Select your preferred date(s).
Click “Submit” and confirm.

5. Can I reserve multiple tickets for each day?
No, you can only reserve one ticket for each day.

6. How do I cancel my reservation?
Click "View Reservation" on the homepage.
Select "Cancel Reservation" and confirm.

7. Can I change my reservation after booking?
No, but you can cancel, sign in again, and make a new reservation with the updated details.

8. How do I know if my reservation is confirmed?
You will see a confirmation message on the website and a confirmation email.

9. What happens if an event is canceled?
You will be notified via email or through the platform with further details.
