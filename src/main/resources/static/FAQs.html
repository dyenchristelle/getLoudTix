GetLoudTix

1. What is GetLoudTix?
GetLoudTix is an online ticket reservation platform for free concerts, allowing users to reserve tickets easily and securely.

2. Do I need an account to make a reservation?
No, but you need to sign in and provide information such as name and email for your identity.

3. How do I make a reservation?
- Click “Start Reservation” on the homepage.
- Sign in
- Select your preferred concert(s).
- Click “Check Out” and confirm.

4. Can I reserve multiple tickets for each concert?
No, you can only reserve one ticket for each concert.

5. How do I cancel my reservation?
- Enter name and email - Click "Delete Reservation" on the Registraion Page.

6. Can I change my reservation after booking?
No, but you can cancel, sign in again, and make a new reservation with the updated details.

7. How do I know if my reservation is confirmed?
You will receive an email confirmation.

8. What happens if an event is canceled?
You will be notified via email.
