How to Use GetLoudTix

MAKING A RESERVATION


1. Click “Get Started” button located at the Homepage and Instructions tab to proceed to the registration page.

2. Enter your name and a verified email address, then click “Start Reservation”.

3. Select your preferred concert(s) by clicking the "Reserve" button.

4. To view your reservations, click the ticket icon located at the upper right corner of the page page.

5. To finalize your reservation, click the “Checkout” button.
If you wish to remove a reservation, select the “Remove” button next to the event you wish to cancel.

6. A confirmation email will be sent to you.

Canceling a Reservation


1. Click “Get Started” button located at the Homepage and Instructions tab to proceed to the registration page.

2. Enter your registered name and a email address.

3. There's a "Delete Reservation" button that you must click to delete.

4. A confirmation email will be sent to you.

Terms and Conditions

1. By reserving a slot, you must provide your name and email. The email address will be used to send a confirmation email regarding the reservation information. You are only limited to one reservation per event day.

2. Tickets are available on a first-come, first-served basis and reservations exceeding the limit will not be accepted.

3. You must ensure the accuracy of your provided information, and check the email for confirmation and updates.
